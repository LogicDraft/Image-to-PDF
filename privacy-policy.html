Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

Image to PDF ("the App") is designed to help users convert images into PDF files on Android devices. We value your privacy and keep data collection to a minimum.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers for PDF conversion.

  • Images you select from gallery are used only to generate PDFs on your device.
  • Photos captured using camera are used only for conversion on your device.
  • Generated PDF files are stored locally on your device.

2. Permissions

  • Camera: Required only if you choose to capture images in the app.
  • Photos/Media access: Required only to select images for conversion.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

4. Internet Usage

The App is built to work offline for core features. Internet connection is not required for creating PDFs.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your phone. Your images and generated PDF files never leave your device unless you manually share them using Android share options. We do not store any data on external servers.

6. Age Restriction

The App is intended for users 13 years of age and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
